2 days of pure joy

Schedule

Day
1

Thursday

20 January 2022

  • 80 km drive(3 hours) and 6 km trek(4 hours, mostly uphill)
  • Arrive at Dehradun Bus/Railway station, where we will be available to greet you at 6:30 a.m.
  • From here we will start our journey towards pantwari through the hills of beautiful hill station Mussoorie, we will reach Pantwari at around 10:00 to 11:00 a.m., this the point where your trek begins.
  • The journey will start after a small safety briefing and will take you approximately 4 hrs to reach Kathian Meadow (base camp for the night) through the beautiful jungle of cedar and rhododendron.
  • Hereafter arrival, Unpack, unwind and inhale the freshness that cities cant afford for you, post allotment of the camps fill your bellies with a luscious meal after which you are free to explore local surrounding and to acclimatize.
  • Enjoy the evening bonfire, with soup and popcorn and spend cosy night at the base camp, Kathian Meadows.
Day
2

Friday

21 January 2022

  • 80 km drive(3 hours) and 16 km trek(8 hours, mostly downhill)
  • Good Morning, feel the fresh and welcoming morning mountain sun!
  • We wrap things up today from Kathian and start our trek to the summit, the trek, we promise shall be nature filled with beautiful rhododendron forests as your surroundings.
  • Dont worry about the steepness, for it shall lead you to the best. On arrival at the summit enjoy the 360-degree view of Bandarpoonch Peak, the Gangotri group of peaks, Kedarnath peak in the north, Doon valley and the snow peaks of Chanabang Himalayas.
  • After spending some time at the Nagtibba top, we descend back to our base camp Kathiyan where we will halt for a while and have our lunch.
  • After a delicious meal, we will start to step down towards Pantwari and reach there by 4:00 p.m.
  • Depart from Pantwari - Dehradun with memories to cherish for! Reach Dehradun by 7 p.m.
Day
3

Saturday

22 January 2022

  • Wake up early to witness the sunrise and warm up to start our trek to Bhalu Gaad Waterfalls (6 km from the resort) at 7 AM.
  • It’s time to learn something new. Join us for workshops which starts at 11 AM.
  • At 2:30 PM, we will start the open mic. Every attendee will get 10 minutes to showcase their talent on stage. We need more independent artists for the next edition, so we hope to see you perform.
  • The performances will happen from 5 PM to 10 PM.
  • Performances stop at 10 PM but the energy doesn’t. Let’s gather around bonfires to jam, make friends and memories.

What is the cancellation policy for the festival?

We totally understand that unforeseen circumstances might need you to change plans, and so our cancellation policy makes it easier to do that.


  • Cancellations prior to 30 days from the start of the trip — full refund.
  • Cancellation between 30 days and 15 days to the start of the trip — 50% refund.
  • Cancellation less than 15 days to the start of the trip — no refund.

    • In case of unforeseen weather conditions, government restrictions or any other unavoidable circumstances beyond the control of the host, certain activities during the expedition or the event itself may be cancelled, or there could be change in the itinerary. In such cases, the host will try their best to provide an alternate feasible activity or a voucher will be handed over (in case of cancellation or rescheduling of event) to you. However no refund will be provided for the same.

    In case the trip is cancelled by the host for lack of minimum number of people in the batch, you will get a full refund.
